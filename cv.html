About Me

I grew up in Bickfaya, Lebanon surrounded by mountains. Being a girl scouts, I spent endless nights starring at the dark skies which aroused my curiosity in Astronomy.

I have a Bachelor degree in Statistics and a Masters degree in Astrophysics. My primary interest lies in the field of exoplanets. I am primarily interested in applying novel statistical methods to better understand the theory of planet formation.
